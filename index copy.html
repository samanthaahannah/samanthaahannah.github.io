About Me

Hello. My name is Samantha Hannah and I am a Digital media graduate as of 2020. I have since been employed as a Technical Artist for a games company and as a Graphic Designer for a small oil & gas company. I have had four years of official training and two years of employment, but I have been building 3D models in my spare time for years prior as a serious hobby doing what I love best.
